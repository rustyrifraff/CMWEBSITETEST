Client Payment Portal

Already have a signed scope with us? Here's how payment works, and where to pay a live invoice securely.

How Billing Works

Three steps, no surprises

  1. Scope is signed off After your intro call and proposal, we agree on a fixed scope, timeline, and price — no open-ended retainers unless you've asked for one.
  2. You receive a secure invoice We send a Stripe-hosted invoice directly to your email. It's a real, secure payment page — we never ask for card details over email or phone.
  3. Pay online, get a receipt instantly Pay by card or bank transfer directly on the invoice page. You'll get an emailed receipt the moment payment clears.

Have an Invoice Number?

Use the secure payment link included in your invoice email from Stripe. If you can't find it, we're happy to resend it.

Go to My Invoice
Payments are processed securely by Stripe. C+M never stores or has access to your card details.
Setup note (remove once live): This page is wired for Stripe but needs your live account connected. Create a free Stripe account at stripe.com, verify your business details, then either (1) turn on Stripe Invoicing and send invoices directly from your dashboard after each quote call — clients will get their own secure payment link by email automatically, or (2) replace the "Go to My Invoice" button's placeholder link with your Stripe Customer Portal or a specific Payment Link. No backend/server is required for either option.
