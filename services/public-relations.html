Public Relations

Media strategy, press positioning, and earned coverage built on two decades of newsroom relationships and editorial instinct.

Most small and mid-sized businesses approach press the way an outsider would — hoping a reporter notices them. We approach it the way an insider does, because we were the insider. Twenty years on the other side of the pitch means we know what makes a story worth covering, what a producer needs to say yes in ninety seconds, and how to shape a narrative that survives contact with a skeptical newsroom.

Public relations at C+M isn't a press-release-and-pray retainer. It's a scoped engagement built around your actual news value — and where that news value doesn't exist yet, we help you build it.

What This Typically Includes

  • Media Strategy & Press PositioningIdentifying the angles, timing, and outlets where your story actually has a chance of landing — and being honest when it doesn't yet.
  • Newsroom-Grade PitchingPitches written and delivered the way a producer or editor actually wants to receive them, not a generic template blasted to a purchased list.
  • Executive Visibility & Media TrainingPreparing founders and leadership to do interviews with confidence — how to stay on message, handle a tough question, and sound like themselves on camera.
  • Earned Coverage MeasurementTracking placements against goals that actually matter to the business, not just a running tally of mentions.
  • Ongoing Press Relationship ManagementMaintaining the relationships that turn a single placement into a recurring source relationship with local and trade press.

  • Newsroom-tested pitching
  • Executive media training
  • Coverage measurement & reporting
