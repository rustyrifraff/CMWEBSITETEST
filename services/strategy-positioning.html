Strategy & Positioning

Go-to-market and narrative strategy that turns a modern digital footprint and SEO presence into qualified leads, not just traffic.

A lot of small and mid-sized businesses have invested in a website, some social presence, maybe even paid ads — and still aren't showing up where their next customer is actually searching, or aren't converting the traffic they do get. That's a strategy and positioning problem, not just a marketing execution problem.

This is where Jared's project-management background shapes the work as much as Shaun's storytelling instinct does: strategy at C+M is scoped, sequenced, and measured like a managed initiative, not a vague ongoing retainer with no clear definition of success.

What This Typically Includes

  • Go-to-Market StrategyA clear plan for how the business gets in front of its actual buyers, sequenced around real budget and bandwidth constraints.
  • Narrative & Positioning FrameworkA defined story of who you are, who you're for, and why you're different — the throughline every other piece of content and outreach should tie back to.
  • SEO-Aligned Digital FootprintMaking sure your website, listings, and content are actually structured for how modern search — including AI-driven search — surfaces businesses like yours.
  • Lead Generation MeasurementDefining and tracking success in terms of qualified leads and business outcomes, not vanity traffic metrics.
  • Ongoing Strategic CounselRegular check-ins to adjust the plan as the market, competitors, or the business itself changes.

At a Glance

  • Go-to-market strategy
  • Narrative & positioning framework
  • SEO-aligned digital footprint
  • Lead-focused measurement
