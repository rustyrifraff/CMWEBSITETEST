Branding & Identity

Visual and verbal identity systems that give small and mid-sized businesses the polish and credibility of a much larger competitor.

A lot of small businesses do genuinely excellent work and still look like an afterthought online — a logo that was never quite finished, messaging that changes depending on who's writing it, a visual identity that doesn't match the quality of what's actually being delivered. That gap is what costs you the client who was comparing you to a bigger, more polished competitor.

Branding at C+M closes that gap — not with a rebrand for its own sake, but with a coherent system your team can actually use consistently, everywhere your business shows up.

What This Typically Includes

  • Visual Identity SystemLogo, color, typography, and imagery guidelines built to hold up across a website, deck, signage, and social — not just a logo file and nothing else.
  • Verbal Identity & MessagingA defined voice and message architecture so your business sounds like the same company no matter who's writing the copy that day.
  • Brand Guidelines & TemplatesA practical reference document and usable templates, so consistency doesn't depend on remembering rules from memory.
  • Competitive PositioningA clear-eyed look at how you're perceived next to competitors with bigger budgets, and what specifically closes that credibility gap.
  • Digital Presence AlignmentMaking sure the brand system actually gets applied to the website and search-facing presence, not just internal documents.

At a Glance

  • Visual identity & guidelines
  • Voice & message architecture
  • Competitive positioning
  • Applied across your digital footprint
