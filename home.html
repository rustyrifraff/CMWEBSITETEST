How Engagements Are Managed

A managed process, not a mystery

Clients who've never worked with an agency deserve to know exactly what happens next. Every engagement follows the same four-phase structure.

01

Discovery

A structured intake covering current positioning, past marketing spend, competitors, and goals — captured in a shared engagement charter.

02

Scope & Proposal

A fixed-scope proposal with milestones, deliverables, and a quote — no open-ended retainers unless you ask for one.

03

Delivery

Weekly checkpoints, a single point of contact, and status reporting modeled on the cadence used in large-scale institutional projects.

04

Review & Handoff

A closeout report tied back to the original goals, plus a clear plan for what (if anything) continues after the engagement ends.
